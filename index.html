I’ve been animating for over a decade now, starting out in Colombia and working across games, film, TV, and ads. I moved from Animator to Tech Anim Senior, always bouncing between the creative side and the technical one, rigging, modular outfits, custom animation tools, and building workflows in Maya and Unity to keep things moving smooth.

Lately I’ve been diving into Unreal, pushing for a style that feels bold and fun, while improving how things work under the hood. I like keeping things clean, punchy, and production-ready.

When I’m not animating, I’m usually out cycling to clear my head. Always open to new challenges and working with people who really care about this craft.
